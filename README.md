# Udemy-Course-Analysis
An evaluation of course subscribers, pricing, and subject-wise performance trends over the years.

CASE STUDY

Objective

To evaluate subscriber trends, course types, pricing structures, and content duration patterns.

3. Free vs Paid Course Performance

-Paid courses have significantly higher subscriber counts.

-Free courses attract higher initial engagement but low conversion.

4. Subject-wise Subscribers

-Web Development attracts the largest number of subscribers.

-Graphic Design, Business Finance, and Music trail behind.

Business Recommendations

-Udemy should invest more in Web Development and Tech courses.

-Provide certification bundles for high-value professional courses.

-Encourage instructors to price courses competitively for developing markets.
